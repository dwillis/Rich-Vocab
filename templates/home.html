Vocabulary Richness Detector

This simple application uses a Python implementation of Yule's I score (the inverse of Yule's K) to determine the diversity of vocabulary present in text. Enter some text into the form below and click the Go! button to see the Yule I score. The higher the score, the richer the vocabulary and, arguably, the more difficult the text.
